Sisters Coffee Table

Inspired by the footprint of the Three Sisters Mountains, and traditional rice farming commonly seen in Thailand. This table utilized Grasshopper and the input function Meta-ball 2D to create a unique formation and adaptable shape. The final product was made through a CNC process embedding the next cut within the previous piece to minimize waste while hollowing out the interior of the table. Assembly is done with screws, and varnish can be applied if desired.
